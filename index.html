Who am I?

I am a Software Engineer with more than 8 years of experience designing and developing scalable digital solutions for web and mobile platforms. Throughout my career, I have worked across industries such as financial services, transportation, and insurance, building high-performance applications focused on reliability, scalability, and user experience. My strongest expertise is backend development using NodeJS and NestJS, where I specialize in designing robust APIs, clean architectures, and scalable microservices. I also have experience working with frontend and mobile technologies including Angular, Flutter, and Swift. Additionally, I hold an iOS Developer Certification from Backbase , which strengthened my expertise in mobile banking solutions and enterprise-level application development. I am passionate about creating modern, efficient, and impactful software solutions while continuously learning and adapting to new technologies and challenges.
